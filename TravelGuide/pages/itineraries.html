Japan in 10 Days: Tokyo to Kyoto

10 Days Asia Culture and Food Moderate Budget

From the neon canyons of Shinjuku to the bamboo serenity of Arashiyama, this itinerary hits every essential while leaving room for discovery. Designed for first-timers who want depth, not just selfies.

Day-by-Day Plan

Days 1-2: Tokyo Arrival

Arrive at Narita or Haneda. Check in to Shinjuku. Explore Shibuya Crossing, Harajuku, and Meiji Shrine. Evening ramen in Shinjuku Golden Gai.

Days 3-4: Tokyo Deep Dive

Senso-ji temple at dawn. TeamLab digital art. Akihabara electronics district. Tsukiji fish market breakfast. Tokyo Skytree at sunset.

Day 5: Mt. Fuji Day Trip

Bullet train to Kawaguchiko. Walk the Fuji Five Lakes. Optional 5th-station hike. Return to Tokyo for farewell dinner.

Days 6-7: Kyoto Temples

Shinkansen to Kyoto. Fushimi Inari at sunrise. Kinkaku-ji, the Golden Pavilion. Gion district at dusk.

Days 8-9: Nara and Arashiyama

Feed the sacred deer of Nara. Visit Todai-ji. Walk the Arashiyama bamboo grove and join a tea ceremony workshop.

Day 10: Osaka and Departure

Dotonbori street food, Osaka Castle, and your departure from Kansai Airport. Sayonara.
